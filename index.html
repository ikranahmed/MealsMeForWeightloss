Start Your Day Off with Push-Ups

Push-ups are a great way to kickstart your morning and build strength. Click below for tips and tricks to perfect your push-up technique and maximize your results!

Push-Ups

  • Start in a plank position with hands shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower your body until your elbows are bent at 90 degrees
  • Then push back up to the starting position, keeping your core tight and body in a straight line.
  • Modify by performing the movement on your knees.
